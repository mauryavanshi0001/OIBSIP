About the Indian Army

The Indian Army is the land-based branch and the largest component of the Indian Armed Forces. It plays a crucial role in ensuring the nation's security and defending its sovereignty.

History and Legacy

The Indian Army has a rich history dating back to ancient times. It has been involved in various wars, conflicts, and peacekeeping missions, showcasing remarkable valor and sacrifice.

Notable Achievements

Throughout its history, the Indian Army has achieved numerous milestones, including successful operations, contributions to nation-building, and global peacekeeping efforts.
